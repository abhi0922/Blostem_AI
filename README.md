# 🚀 Blostem AI Engine

An AI-powered B2B marketing and sales intelligence platform designed to help enterprises identify high-intent prospects, automate personalized outreach, and accelerate deal closure.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

B2B sales cycles in enterprises (banks, fintech platforms) are:
- Long and complex  
- Involve multiple stakeholders  
- Lack personalization in outreach  
- Suffer from low conversion rates  

After onboarding, activation often stalls due to poor targeting and inefficient follow-ups.

---

## 💡 Solution

Blostem AI Engine solves this by:

- Identifying **high-intent enterprise leads** using market signals  
- Automating **personalized outreach sequences**  
- Ensuring **compliance-safe communication**  
- Providing **sales intelligence** on who to contact and when  

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔍 Lead Intelligence Engine
- AI-based lead scoring  
- Detects signals like hiring trends, funding, fintech activity  

### 👥 Multi-Stakeholder Mapping
- Identifies key roles:
  - CTO  
  - Product Head  
  - Compliance Officer  

### 📧 Outreach Automation
- AI-generated personalized emails  
- Multi-step sequences (follow-ups included)  

### 🛡️ Compliance Engine
- Flags risky or non-compliant messaging  
- Ensures safe communication for fintech domain  

### 📊 Sales Intelligence Dashboard
- Lead prioritization  
- Engagement probability  
- Best time to contact  
- Campaign performance metrics
